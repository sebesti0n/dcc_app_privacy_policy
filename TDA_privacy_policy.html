Information Collection

Types of Data Collected:

The DCC App collects the following information:

  • Details of events organized by Developers and Coders Club.

How We Use Collected Information:

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

We do not share any collected information with third parties unless required by law or for the purpose of facilitating the events and services provided by Developers and Coders Club. Any sharing of information will be done with explicit consent from the users.

No Third-Party Sharing: The DCC App does not sell or share any user data with third parties.
